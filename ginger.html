About our Dry Ginger

We bring you ASTA-grade dry ginger from some of the largest ginger producing farms in Kaduna, Nigeria.

Our Dry Ginger

Dry Split Ginger Our Dry Ginger is well known for culinary, medicinal and spice purpose and we ensure the complete processing of cultivation of ginger and later drying it through our tedious and hygienic processes. The dried root or dried ginger is available as whole or sliced. Further the dry ginger is specially packed to retain its freshness, flavour, nutrients and aroma for a longer time.

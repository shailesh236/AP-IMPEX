Specifications

Cashew Nuts are native to subtropical climate but is widely available across the globe. This tiny kidney bean shaped nut is the powerhouse of an array of nutrients. Our Cashews are subject to following specifications: Nut count: 180-200, kor: 48-53, Moisture: 10% max, defective pieces 12% max, total fat: 4.38g

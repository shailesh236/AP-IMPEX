Our Exotic Clove

Clove( Syzygium aromaticum) is a natural herb used in our day to day life. It has potential health benefits which are yet to be explored. Clove (Syzygium aromaticum) is the most valuable spices that has been used for centuries as food preservative and for many medicinal purposes. Clove is native of Indonesia but now a days is cultured in several parts of the world. This plant represents one of the richest source of phenolic compounds such as eugenol ,eugenol acetate and gallic acid and possess great potential for pharmaceutical, cosmetic, food and agricultural applications.
